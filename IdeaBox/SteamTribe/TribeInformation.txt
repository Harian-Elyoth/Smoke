The Steam Tribe (white) comes from the State of the Art Citadel of BelWard
Long time ago, The Belward Dwarf made a lot of automaton to help them at work.
To Greedy the dwarf claimed the treasure of the BlackRock Mountain. They didn't expect The Burned and hostile creature in there.
The dwarf people were killed letting the Automaton rules the city.


The Steam Tribe use the Steam Core Field as the main Card in the deck
The purpose is to generate a lot of steam to spawn epic SteamPunk Creature
With great offense and deffense stats. 
The Steam Core generate a lot of Steam per turn enhanced by steam Creature.
