The Dust Tribe comes from the Nahaja Desert. These hostile creature hidden behind the dunes wait the next lost adventurer.
We don't know a lot about this tribe instead that their hunger never end.

The Dust tribe feed themselves eating opponents and their own dead soldiers. The Graveyard CardList is core of their gameplay.
The Dust tribe purpose is to have plenty of dead creature in the graveyard to boost their own creature. And attacking a lot.
The gameplay of the deck is based of the discard. The effect of all the Dust card became stronger depending on the number of creature in your graveyard.
